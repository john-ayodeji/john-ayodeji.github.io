The Student Identity Network

The Problem

Every day, students face the same frustrating challenges: proving they're students to access discounts, managing multiple campus IDs, and dealing with fragmented verification systems. Whether it's showing a physical ID card, uploading documents repeatedly, or missing out on perks because verification is too complicated, the current system is broken.

Universities, businesses, and service providers all struggle with the same issue from the other side: how do you verify student status efficiently, securely, and at scale?

Our Mission

We're building CampusID.me to solve this once and for all. One verified digital identity that works everywhere. No more uploading documents. No more expired IDs. No more missed opportunities. Just seamless, secure verification that puts students in control.
